Who We Are?

We believe that protecting the langur means protecting life itself. The Lutong Conservation Project was born from Belitung’s collective spirit to defend forests, traditions, and wildlife. Through research and community collaboration, we strive to restore the bond between people and nature for future generations.

What We Do?

Research and Education

We advance conservation science and share environmental knowledge with local communities, turning research into real action.

Community Empowerment

We empower communities through sustainable livelihoods, ensuring that protecting nature also supports local well‑being.

Advocacy and Collaboration

We strengthen conservation policies and build networks that unite stakeholders to safeguard Belitung’s forests and wildlife.
